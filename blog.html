The White Wood Aster

2019

Characterized by their spray-like clusters and bright yellow disks, these white asters provide a vital late-season nectar source. The dense, layered composition highlights the "wild" beauty of meadow flora.

The Luminous Archive

2018

This curated assembly captures the ephemeral beauty of the "in-between" moments. Through a soft-focus lens and high-contrast lighting, the subject matter moves from the botanical to the ethereal.

Signage & Shadow

2024

Cinematic and assertive, this black-and-white capture transforms a commercial storefront into a graphic masterpiece. The interplay between the radiant lightbox and the deep, ink-black shadows creates a visual tension.

Equilibrium in Monochrome

2020

A poignant study of balance and perseverance captured from a fleeting perspective. In this frame, the solitary figure becomes a living sculpture, moving through a landscape of elongated shadows and dappled light.
